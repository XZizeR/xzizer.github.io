Bakery Recipes

That's the first wee see when you land in the main page of the web app.

We can search recipes from over 1 million recipes stored in a remote server. Type the recipe name, click on search, choose from the recipe menu and then you have an image and all the ingredients you need to make it.
Awesome right?

At the chosen recipe details we see the time it takes to make it, the number of people it serves, and a heart for saving our recipe for later. Could be cool if we could increase or decrease the number of people we are preparing this recipe for, and the ingredients units will change proportionally, right? that's exactly how it works here!
We also can save as many recipes as we like to the favorite list, and it stores them in our pc local storage, which means if we decide to get back, they will be there.

Now under the recipe ingredients, we have a button to save the following ingredients to the shopping list or to move to the original web page of the recipe. The shopping list is the summary of all of the recipes ingredients we added before.
