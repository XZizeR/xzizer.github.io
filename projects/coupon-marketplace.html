Coupon Marketplace

The Coupon Marketplace web application. Build and deployed mostly by Spring Boot application framework, Angular web framework, and Bootstrap front-end framework with the help of Lombok, Hibernate, RESTful Web services and database management service MySQL.

Looking forward to Dockerize the application with a separately running MySQL container.

When we land on the coupon marketplace main page, we land straight to the login section. It's a closed application, which means we have to login in order to use it, and only the admin can create us an account.

Jump to:
  • admin
  • company
  • customer

    • The 'Back' button pressed, and the 'Sign-Out' not pressed. Every signed-in user account has a 'Back' and a 'Sign-out' button in their upper panel. When we click on the 'Back', we move to the page we have been to before. If we keep pressing 'Back' we will move pages backward to the first user welcome page, no further.

    Previous Next

    When we sign-in as admin, we first of all, getting welcomed as an admin account, and as the one we get to see a panel with a bunch of options we can do with customer and company accounts. In a total of 10 options, 5 of them are for the customers, and 5 of the rest are for the companies. These options are adding, updating, deleting, finding one, and getting the whole list for each of the account types.

  • For adding customer or company accounts we have to fill in all fields. Filling in every field here is essential.
  • To update a customer or a company, doing by filling in an id number and new user information. As we said before, when we adding a user, filling in every field is essential, the same is here.
  • To delete a customer or a company from the system, by filling in the id number of the same user we would like to delete. Be cautious, deleted user cannot be restored, he's deleted permanently with all the coupons in his and, in a company case, other users possession.
  • We can get a customer or a company user account information, without the need of looking for him in the list of all users myself, all I need to do is fill in his id number and I will find him.
  • In the 'All' option we get the list of the whole customers or companies user accounts details.

    Previous Next

    When we sign-in as a company, we got to see a panel of 6 options we can do with coupons. The options are to add, update, delete, coupons, coupons sorted with categories, coupons limited by price, and company details.

  • To add a coupon we have to fill in all the fields.
  • To update a coupon we have to fill in a coupon id number and new coupon information. Filling every field is essential, and the dates have to be consistent, start date before the end date.
  • To delete a coupon from the system, fill in the coupon id number of the same coupon we would like to delete. Warning, deleting a coupon will delete the same coupon from all the users in the system, and they cannot be restored, they are deleted permanently.
  • To check-out our coupons, we choose 'my coupons', where we can see all our companies coupons.
  • To check-out our coupons sorted with categories, we choose "my coupons by categories", where we can see all our coupons sorted by chosen categories.
  • To check-out our coupons limited by price, we choose "my coupons by price", where we can see all our coupons limited by the chosen price.
  • In company details, we see the details of our company stored in the system.

    Previous Next

    When we sign-in as a customer, we got to see a panel of 6 various options we can do with the coupons. The options are to purchase, all coupons, my coupons, my coupons sorted with categories, my coupons limited by price, and customer details.

  • To purchase a coupon we have to type the coupon id number, we can check the id number at the "all coupons" option.
  • In all coupons option, we see all the coupons of all the companies in the system. In this option, we can check the coupon id number, name, description, quantity, price, start to end dates and pictures.
  • In my coupons option, we can check all the coupons the customer purchased.
  • In my coupons by category option, we can check all the coupons the customer purchased sorted with categories.
  • In my coupons by price option, we can check all the coupons the customer purchased sorted by price limit.
  • At customer details, we can check our customers details in the system. There we can check the customer id number, first and last name, password.
